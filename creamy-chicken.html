Creamy Chicken and Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

This creamy chicken and mashed potatoes dish is easy, uber-tasty, and cozy. It’s simple yet packs a big flavor in every bite. With its juicy chicken tidbits, rich potatoes, and creamy sauce, this easy creamy chicken and mashed potatoes recipe is definitely picky-eater-approved.

WHY YOU’LL LOVE THIS RECIPE

  • Easy– This creamy chicken and mashed potatoes dish only requires a few kitchen tools/appliances.
  • Flavorful – White wine, seasoning, dijon, and parmesan cheese come together to create a deliciously decadent sauce.
  • Creamy– The secret to this yummy dish is the heavy cream, which helps create a rich sauce.
  • Filling– The mashed potatoes in this recipe make it more satisfying and filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin oil
  • 3 tablespooms of salted butter
  • kosher salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 4 ounces finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups of whole milk ricotta cheese

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, toss the chicken, salt, paprika, garlic, and pepper until completely coated.
  2. In a large skillet melt the butter over medium high heat. Sear the chicken for 3-5 minutes, depending on thickness.
  3. Flip and sear for another 3-5 minutes or until the chicken is mostly cooked through. Remove from the pan.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium low. Do not wipe out the pan or remove any of the grease. Add the shallot. Sauté for 4-5 minutes or until softened.
  5. Add the garlic, flour, and italian seasoning. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Deglaze the pan with white wine, scraping any brown bits off the bottom of the pan
  7. Add the cream, water, and mustard. Stir until combined.
  8. Stir in the parmesan cheese.
  9. Add the chicken and any juices back into the sauce and simmer until cooked through and warmed. Serve the chicken over the mashed potatoes.
