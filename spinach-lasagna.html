Spinach Lasagna

Description

Kicking Monday off with my White Pesto Spinach Lasagna. Simply the BEST. Layers of creamy cheese bechamel sauce with basil pesto, spinach, and caramelized mushrooms. This dinner is always a favorite with our Christmas crew. It’s a great meal for entertaining, just make it ahead of time, and bake it off before guests arrive. Creamy, cheesy, spinach lasagna flavored with a hint of nutmeg and a combination of besciamella (that's Italian for "white sauce") and fresh ricotta. While I'll often opt for the ease and convenience of no-boil lasagna noodles, today we're going to go with store-bought fresh pasta.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin oil
  • 3 tablespooms of salted butter
  • kosher salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 4 ounces finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups of whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 2 pounds fresh flat or curly spinach leaves, washed and dried
  • 1 1/2 pounds (enough sheets to make 12 layers) fresh lasagna noodles

Steps:

  1. 1.Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. 2. Heat the olive oil In a large skillet over medium heat. When the oil shimmers, add the mushrooms and season with salt and pepper. Cook undisturbed for 5 minutes or until golden, stir and continue cooking until the mushrooms have caramelized, 3-5 minutes. Remove the mushrooms from the skillet to a plate.
  3. 3. Add the butter, garlic and oregano, salt, and pepper and cook 30 seconds or until fragrant. Whisk in the flour and cook for about 1 minute. Add the milk and broth. Bring to a boil and stir for 1 minute. Remove from heat and stir in the Raclette cheese and 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth. Set the cheese sauce aside.
  4. 4. In a medium bowl combine the ricotta, spinach, and pesto.
  5. 5. Spread 1/4 of the cheese sauce in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Top with 3-4 lasagna sheets. Spread with 1/2 the ricotta cheese mixture, another 1/4 of the cheese sauce, and half of the mushrooms. Place another 3-4 lasagna noodles on top and then top with the remaining ricotta cheese mixture, another 1/4 of the cheese sauce, and the remaining mushrooms. Add the remaining lasagna noodles and pour the remaining cheese sauce over top. Top with a 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes or until the top has bubbled up and browned a bit. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Butter Chicken Pasta

Description

This Butter Chicken Pasta is my twist on an Indian classic. The pasta is tossed in a rich spiced tomato cream sauce and is loaded with tender chicken thighs. Everything you love about a classic butter chicken, but swap the rice for pasta. It all comes together in only about 30 minutes too!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • ginger
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 chopped onion
  • garam masala powder
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • 1/2 cup of parsley
  • 1 pack of fusilli pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • spices: salt, pepper, cayenne, cumin, tumeric

Before you start!

  • Cut the chicken. Slice the chicken thighs into bite size pieces.
  • Press the garlic. I love pressing garlic for sauces. It brings out the flavor. Plus this garlic press makes it quick and easy.

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium high heat. Add the chicken, salt, pepper, garam masala, chili powder, cumin, turmeric, and cayenne. Sauté until the chicken is mostly cooked through. Turn the heat to medium low.
  2. Add the onion and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the tomato, honey, ginger, and garlic. Sauté for another minute.
  4. Add cream. Scrape any brown bits off the bottom of the pan. Bring to a simmer. Cover and cook on low for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. While the sauce simmer, boil the pasta according to the instructions on the package. Reserve about 1-2 cups of pasta water.
  6. Toss the pasta with sauce. Add about ½ cup of pasta water to start. Thin the sauce with more as needed.
  7. Sprinkle the parsley on top
